============================================================
1. BASIC INFORMATION
============================================================

-------------------------
Visa Requirements (India)
-------------------------
- Indian passport holders require a tourist/visit visa before travel 
  (unless eligible for visa-on-arrival).
- Processing time: 3–5 working days through online portal or airline/agent.
- Required documents:
  - Passport with at least 6 months validity
  - Passport-sized photograph
  - Confirmed flight tickets
  - Hotel booking confirmation

-----------------
Travel Insurance
-----------------
- Must include:
  - Health coverage
  - Emergency evacuation
  - Trip cancellation
  - Lost luggage
  - Flight delays
- Recommended to buy immediately after booking flights.

-------------------
Airport Transfers
-------------------
- Options:
  - Pre-booked private transfer
  - Hotel shuttle
  - Airport taxi
  - Uber / Careem
- Travel time from DXB Airport to hotel: 30–60 minutes depending on location.

-----------------------
Local Transportation
-----------------------
- Dubai Metro
- Local buses
- Taxis
- Uber / Careem
- Nol Card for metro and bus travel.

-----------------------
Time Zone & Currency
-----------------------
- Dubai: UTC+4
- Bangalore: UTC+5:30
- Currency: UAE Dirham (AED)
